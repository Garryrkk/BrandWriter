Forbidden Words (Never Use)

cutting-edge

industry-leading

next-gen

revolutionary

innovative

transformative

unlock potential

supercharge

digital transformation

synergy

robust solutions

world-class

future-ready

Overused Startup Phrases to Avoid

“AI is the future”

“In today’s changing landscape…”

“We leverage AI…”

“We empower businesses…”

“We revolutionize workflows…”

Preferred Phrasing

Be specific, not dramatic.

Show the outcome, not the hype.

Replace adjectives with evidence.

Examples:
❌ “next-gen evaluation pipeline”
✅ “evaluation you can run in minutes, not hours”
